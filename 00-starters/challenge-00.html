UFO Invaders: The Game


    Backstory: 
    The presence of aliens visiting our planet and abducting
    small animals has been a known fact for sometime. But their master 
    plan is to colonize our planet in a hostile takeover.  In this game
    the attack begins.  Fortunately an alien defense system exists to
    prevent the alients from bringing death and destruction.
    

    Here's how the game will work:
    

    The aliens win if they get 10 visitors to land on the earth.
    This is their magic number required to overpower the planet. 
    

    The humans win if they can successfully destroy the UFO.
    

    The UFO will begin with 5 energy. 
    It will lose one energy each time the earth's defense system hits it with a missile.
    However, it will gain one energy each time it successfully abducts a small animal.

    The earth's defense system will be equipped with a missile launcher used to try and
    destroy the UFO. It will also be equipped with an abduction shield to block the
    UFO from consuming small animals. Like cats.
    

    However, if the abduction beam hits the defense system three times, then it is
    destroyed and it's game over for the humans.
    

    The beam moves fast. But it does give a three second warning. So the humans will
    need to act quick to get in position and activate the shields. Or stay out of the way!
